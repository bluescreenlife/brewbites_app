Featured Brewery: Forgotten Star Brewing

Founded in 2019 in Fridley, Minnesota, Forgotten Star Brewing was forged from a building rich with history and defined by Excellence and Efficiency. Today, they incorporate these values into every aspect of their brewery, from their brewing process and taproom space, to their people, atmosphere and beer.

Featured Food Truck: HodgePodge Eats

Their menu is a Midwest-inspired, food-for-all kind of menu. This shines through the variety of menu items, unique midwestern ingredients, and approachable price-point. You’ll find satisfying burgers, healthy-ish appetizers, and plenty of shareables. And if you’re a beer nerd, they have recommended Forgotten Star tap parings for each menu item. They even have a kids menu and a soft-serve machine!
