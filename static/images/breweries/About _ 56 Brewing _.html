At 56 Brewing we take diversity, equity, and inclusion seriously. We are committed to building a team and environment with a variety of backgrounds and views. We strive to be a place where people can gather and feel included and valued regardless of race, gender, ethnicity, or sexual orientation. Our goal is to inspire awareness and encourage others to embrace each other’s uniqueness. Together we have the power to make a difference and create a world that is equitable for all.

For us, craft beer is brewed to strengthen community. It’s brewed to host a new conversation. It’s brewed for everyone, and it pairs best with intentional actions.

From Minneapolis to the world:

We’re 56 Brewing, and we’re crafting community one beer at a time.
